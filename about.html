Our Story

A legacy of beauty spanning over 60 years

A Timeless Tradition

The Velvet Comb opened its doors in 1962, during the golden age of glamorous hairstyling. Founded by Margaret Beaumont, a visionary stylist who trained in the finest salons of Paris and New York, our salon has been a beacon of classic beauty for generations.

Margaret believed that every woman deserved to feel like a Hollywood starlet, and she brought that philosophy to life with every style she created. From elegant updos to sophisticated waves, she mastered the art of vintage hairstyling and passed her knowledge down through generations of stylists.

Today, The Velvet Comb continues Margaret's legacy, blending time-honored techniques with contemporary flair. We honor the past while embracing the present, ensuring that each client leaves our salon feeling confident, beautiful, and utterly timeless.
